# Some Considerations for Replayability

- Have multiple story templates, not just one
- Implement player choices (even for small things, like the player events). Make sure to not include conjecture.
- Use descriptive language and sensory details for the "random" events. Maybe AI can help. - Example: The dusty road stretches ahead. A merchant's weathered cart sits at the roadside,
  its owner eyeing you with a mix of hope and suspicion. "Looking for supplies,
  friend? Or perhaps... something more valuable?" - Instead of "You see a merchant down the road. Do you want to buy something? [yes/no]
